**Workout Name: Leg_Blaster_15**

**Duration:** 15 Minutes
**Focus:** Legs
**Equipment:** None

---

**Warm-up (2 minutes)**
*   **Jumping Jacks:** 30 seconds
*   **Leg Swings (forward/backward):** 30 seconds per leg
*   **Dynamic Calf Stretches:** 30 seconds

**Workout (11 minutes) - Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, followed by 20 seconds of rest. Complete 3 rounds with a 60-second rest between rounds.**

**Round 1**
1.  **Jump Squats:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
2.  **Alternating Lunges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
3.  **Glute Bridges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
    *   **REST:** 60 seconds

**Round 2**
1.  **Jump Squats:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
2.  **Alternating Lunges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
3.  **Glute Bridges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
    *   **REST:** 60 seconds

**Round 3**
1.  **Jump Squats:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
2.  **Alternating Lunges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest
3.  **Glute Bridges:** 40 seconds work, 20 seconds rest

**Cool-down (2 minutes)**
*   **Standing Quad Stretch:** 30 seconds per leg
*   **Standing Hamstring Stretch:** 30 seconds per leg
