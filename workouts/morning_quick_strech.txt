**Workout Name: 5-Minute Morning Stretch**

**Duration:** 5 Minutes
**Focus:** Full Body Stretching
**Equipment:** None

---

**Stretching Routine (Perform each stretch for 30 seconds):**

1.  **Neck Tilts:** Gently tilt your head to one side, bringing your ear towards your shoulder. Hold for 30 seconds, then switch sides.
2.  **Shoulder Rolls:** Roll your shoulders forward for 15 seconds, then backward for 15 seconds.
3.  **Cat-Cow Stretch:** Get on all fours. Arch your back and lift your head (cow), then round your back and tuck your chin (cat). Flow between these for 30 seconds.
4.  **Seated Forward Fold:** Sit with your legs extended in front of you. Hinge at your hips and reach towards your toes. Hold for 30 seconds.
5.  **Figure-Four Stretch:** Lie on your back, bend your knees, and place your feet flat on the floor. Cross one ankle over the opposite knee. Gently pull the bottom leg towards your chest. Hold for 30 seconds per side.
6.  **Standing Calf Stretch:** Stand facing a wall, place your hands on it. Step one foot back, keeping both heels on the ground and front knee slightly bent. Lean forward to feel the stretch in your calf. Hold for 30 seconds per side.
