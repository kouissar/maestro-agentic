### 10-Minute At-Home Abs Workout
**Instructions**: Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, then move to the next. Complete 2 rounds with a 30-second rest between rounds.
**Round 1 & 2**
1. **Crunches** (30 seconds)
2. **Plank** (30 seconds)
3. **Leg Raises** (30 seconds)
4. **Russian Twists** (30 seconds)
5. **Bicycle Crunches** (30 seconds)
**Rest for 30 seconds between rounds.**
